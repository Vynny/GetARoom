Help

If you're having trouble navigating the website, please refer to the following instructions below:

1-Select the room you wish to book from the left side panel.

2-Select the day, then click on "Reserve This Room" on the top right corner of the calendar.

3-Select the time slot by clicking & dragging over the calendar view of the selected day. If you make a mistake, the "Reserve" button will warn you before submitting.

4-If your selection is valid, the button will turn green and display as "Reserved". Click it to confirm your reservation.

5-You will be redirected to the calendar view for this room, where you should see the reservation under your name.

1-Click on the "Profile" button on the top right of any page.

2-You profile page lists the reservations made under your name. Click modify reservation under the Actions column for the reservation you wish to modify.

3-Click and drag the center of the reservation if you just wish to move the starting time, while conserving the interval period.

4-Click and drag the bottom of the reservation to extend the interval period of the reservation.

5-If you make a mistake, the "Reserve" button will warn you before submitting.

6-If your modification is valid,the button will turn green and display as "Confirm Modification". Click it to finish modifying your reservation.
