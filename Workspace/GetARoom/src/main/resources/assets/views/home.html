Get a Room is a web application made for your convenience used by Concordia for the purpose of administrating room reservations. The rooms displayed on the left of the screen can be reserved by you as long as the spot is free! If not, you will be placed on a waiting list. Be wary, you might not be the only one waiting in line for the same spot! We advise reserving time slots for rooms well in advance.
