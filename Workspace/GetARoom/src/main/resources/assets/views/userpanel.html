Careful!

Modifications can apply the same restrictions as creating a new reservation.


If you wish to extend your reservation time, your request may be blocked if and only if the new reservation would overlap with an already existing one (from yourself or another user), or if it is outside of the room's operating hours.


If you reduce the time of your existing reservation, it may free a queued reservation from another user for the newly freed time slot. You may not recover that time slot in this scenario, it will be automatically granted to other the user who placed the request, but you may place yourself in the reservation queue for it afterwards.
